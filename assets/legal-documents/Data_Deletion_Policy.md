Data Deletion Instructions for Marquee-Magic

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of all data associated with your Marquee-Magic account at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion
    Send an email to support@marquee-magic.com.
    Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
    In the body of the email, please include the name and email address associated with your Facebook account so we can identify your data.

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete all of your account data from our systems within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
