Privacy Policy for Marquee-Magic

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Marquee-Magic ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our application and the services we provide.

Information We Collect
    When you connect your Facebook account to Marquee-Magic, we may collect the following information provided by Facebook:
        Your name, email address, and public profile information.
        A list of Facebook Pages and linked Instagram Business Accounts that you manage.
        An access token required to publish content to your selected Pages or accounts on your behalf.

How We Use Your Information
    We use the information we collect solely to:
        Authenticate your account.
        Enable you to post images and text from the Marquee-Magic application directly to the Facebook Pages and/or Instagram accounts you select.
        Provide and improve our services.
        We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@marquee-magic.com.
