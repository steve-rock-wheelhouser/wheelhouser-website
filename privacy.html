Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Wheelhouser LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our website, applications, and the services we provide.

General Principles

At Wheelhouser LLC, we believe in privacy by design. Most of our applications are native tools that process your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal files, images, or data to our servers unless explicitly required for a specific service you have opted into (such as social media integration).

Information We Collect

When you use specific features, such as the Meta Platform integration in Marquee-Magic, we may collect the following information provided by the platform:

  • Your name, email address, and public profile information.
  • A list of Facebook Pages and linked Instagram Business Accounts that you manage.
  • An access token required to publish content to your selected Pages or accounts on your behalf.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate your account.
  • Enable you to post images and text from our applications directly to the platforms you select.
  • Provide and improve our services.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Access tokens are stored securely and used only for the purposes you have authorized.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at steve.rock@wheelhouser.com.
