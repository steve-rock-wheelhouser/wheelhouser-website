About Wheelhouser LLC

Founded in 2024, Wheelhouser LLC is a small, independent software company dedicated to creating high-quality, user-friendly applications. Our focus is on building tools that are both powerful and intuitive, designed to solve real-world problems for creatives, developers, and everyday users.

Our philosophy is simple: software should work for you. We believe in privacy, which is why our applications are designed to run entirely on-device, without needing to send your data to the cloud. We are passionate about open-source and contribute to the community whenever we can.

From digital signage solutions to handy utilities for image manipulation and development, our goal is to craft software that you'll love to use.
