About Wheelhouser LLC

Engineering the next generation of native tools.

Who We Are

Wheelhouser LLC is a software development company dedicated to bringing the power of native applications back to the forefront of personal computing. In an era dominated by web apps and subscription services, we stand for software that you truly own and control.

Founded with a passion for performance and privacy, we specialize in building robust utilities and creative tools using modern technologies like Python, Qt, GTK4, Libadwaita, and on-device AI models.

Our Philosophy

  • Privacy First: We believe your data belongs to you. Our tools process files locally on your machine, ensuring that sensitive images and documents never leave your control.
  • Native Performance: We build applications that integrate deeply with your operating system, offering superior performance, system consistency, and offline capabilities that web apps simply cannot match.
  • Sustainable Software: We reject the trend of "software as a service" for basic utilities. Our tools are designed to be reliable, efficient, and respectful of your system resources.

Our Experience

With deep expertise in Linux, Windows, and macOS development, Wheelhouser LLC bridges the gap between professional-grade functionality and user-friendly design. Our portfolio ranges from complex digital signage ecosystems like Marquee-Magic to essential daily utilities for image processing and content creation.

We are committed to the open-source community and actively contribute to the ecosystems we build upon.
