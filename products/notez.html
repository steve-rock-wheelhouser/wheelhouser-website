About the Application

Notez is a lightweight note-taking application designed for speed and simplicity. Keep your thoughts organized without the complexity of full-blown word processors.

Whether you need to jot down a quick idea, draft a todo list, or keep snippets of code, Notez provides a distraction-free environment that gets out of your way.

Key Features

  • Fast & Lightweight: Opens instantly so you never miss an idea.
  • Local Storage: Your notes stay on your device for maximum privacy.
  • Markdown Support: Format your notes with ease using standard Markdown syntax.
  • Distraction-Free: Clean interface focused purely on your content.
