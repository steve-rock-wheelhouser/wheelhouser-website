About the Application

There is nothing worse than a perfect photo ruined by a stray power line, a trash can, or a photobomber. Image Inpainter gives you the power to magically remove these distractions as if they were never there.

Using advanced on-device AI, simply paint over the unwanted object, and the software intelligently fills in the gap using the surrounding textures and details. It is the fastest way to clean up your photography without opening a complex editor.

Key Features

  • Magic Eraser: Remove people, text, watermarks, and blemishes instantly.
  • Simple Brush Tool: Just highlight the area you want to remove and click "Inpaint."
  • Compare View: Toggle between original and processed versions to verify perfection.
  • Privacy First: All AI processing happens locally on your GPU/CPU. No cloud uploads.
