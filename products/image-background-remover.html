About the Application

Stop sending your private photos to the cloud just to remove a background. Image Background Remover is a powerful native desktop application that uses machine learning (RemBG) to intelligently isolate subjects from their background directly on your computer.

Whether you are processing product photos for e-commerce or creating assets for a design project, this tool offers professional-grade masking without the subscription fees or privacy concerns of web-based tools.

Key Features

  • 100% On-Device: No internet connection required. Your images never leave your machine.
  • Batch Processing: Drag and drop an entire folder of images to process them all at once.
  • Smart Masking: Handles complex edges like hair and fur with high precision.
  • Native GTK4 Interface: Fast, fluid, and integrates perfectly with your desktop theme.
