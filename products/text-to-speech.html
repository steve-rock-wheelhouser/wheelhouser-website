About the Application

Gone are the days of robotic, choppy computer voices. Text to Speech leverages the powerful Microsoft Edge Neural TTS engine to generate incredibly lifelike, human-sounding audio directly from your text.

Ideal for content creators, accessibility needs, or simply listening to long documents, this application allows you to generate professional voiceovers without expensive monthly subscriptions. Simply type your text, choose from a wide range of global voices, and export the audio instantly.

Key Features

  • Neural Voices: Access high-fidelity, emotion-aware voices that sound indistinguishable from human speakers.
  • Audio Export: Save your generated speech directly to MP3 files for use in videos or podcasts.
  • Fine-Tuning: Adjust rate (speed), pitch, and volume to get the exact delivery you need.
  • Global Support: Choose from dozens of languages and accents to reach a worldwide audience.
