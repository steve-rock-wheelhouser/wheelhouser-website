About the Application

Preparing images for the web or social media shouldn't take all day. Image Resizer is a high-performance utility designed to process entire folders of photos in seconds.

Whether you need to create thumbnails for a website, compress photos for email, or standardize assets for a project, Image Resizer gives you granular control over dimensions, quality, and file formats without the complexity of a full photo editor.

Key Features

  • Batch Processing: Drag and drop thousands of images and resize them all in one click.
  • Smart Cropping: Automatically center and crop images to fit specific aspect ratios.
  • Format Conversion: Convert seamlessly between JPG, PNG, WEBP, and BMP.
  • Quality Control: Adjust compression levels to find the perfect balance between file size and clarity.
